Rules

    How to Earn Points

  1. Earn points by completing Online Video Courses, Books, and Website Tutorials.
  2. Modifiers will be added based on the speed of completion of courses, books, and tutorials
  3. You will be required to present your project and verify completion of any courses for points to be added to the Highscore Board

How to Validate Points

Video Courses | Youtube Tutorials
  1. Certificate of Completion with a selfie if sent remotely
  2. Bring Certificate of Completion to sub-group meeting
  3. Present the resulting project to the sub-group within 2 minutes
  4. Explain Topic of the Course to the sub-group
Books | Website Tutorials
  1. Bring resulting project to the club meeting
  2. Present the Project
  3. Explain book project or topic
Personal Projects
  1. Explain you current significant commits to the project
  2. Show commit history to verify change
  3. Maintian Consistant Commits between meeting
